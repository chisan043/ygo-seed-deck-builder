# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Yu-Gi-Oh players who want to build, compare, and export playable deck lists quickly. They use the tool while researching archetypes, checking banlist status, and moving between Master Duel, OCG, and TCG contexts.

## Product Purpose

The product turns a seed card or archetype into a deck-building workspace with trend evidence, card imagery, banlist status, export actions, and construction insights. Success means the interface feels trustworthy enough for competitive research while staying fast to scan.

## Brand Personality

Premium, tactical, arcane.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic pale SaaS dashboards, toy-like anime fan pages, unofficial logo imitation, character-art collage, overdecorated fantasy frames, and low-contrast neon-on-black interfaces.

## Design Principles

- Make card images and deck data the visual center.
- Use Untapped-style contrast: warm charcoal field, precise panels, blue/green action colors, and restrained orange emphasis.
- Keep controls familiar and dense so users can stay in the deck-building flow.
- Signal Yu-Gi-Oh influence through card-frame geometry, banlist markers, and tactical HUD details rather than copied IP assets.
- Prefer sharp hierarchy and readable data over spectacle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard focus states, avoid motion that blocks reading, and provide reduced-motion behavior for decorative effects.
